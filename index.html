"Having a nationality is something so natural that people rarely stop to think about what life would be like without it. But at least 10 million people worldwide have no nationality. Moreover, most of these 10 million people are stateless by no fault of their own. Statelessness, not having a nationality, occurs because of discrimination against certain groups, redrawing of borders, and gaps in nationality laws."
